About Us

We are Taking Small Steps to Make Earth Better Planet of the universe

We welcome you with great joy to the Word Liberation Ministry Church. We are here to share the great news of Jesus Christ to all that will listen.
The church has historically been involved in liberation movements, advocating for the freedom and dignity of oppressed peoples. Liberation theology, for instance, emerged in the 20th century, emphasizing the liberation of the poor and marginalized as central to Christian faith. It underscores the church''s role in addressing social injustices, economic inequalities, and systemic oppression. Through its teachings and actions, the church seeks to empower individuals and communities to break free from bondage and experience liberation in all its forms – spiritual, social, and economic.

Tithe

Tithing, or giving a tenth of one's income, has its roots in the Old Testament, particularly in the books of Leviticus, Numbers, and Deuteronomy. In Malachi 3:10, it says, "Bring the whole tithe into the storehouse, that there may be food in my house. Test me in this,” says the Lord Almighty, “and see if I will not throw open the floodgates of heaven and pour out so much blessing that there will not be room enough to store it."

Thankgivings

Thanksgiving is encouraged throughout the Bible as an expression of gratitude to God for His blessings. In Psalm 100:4, it says, "Enter his gates with thanksgiving and his courts with praise; give thanks to him and praise his name."

Bible Study

Studying the Bible is encouraged throughout scripture as a means of growing in faith and understanding God's word. In 2 Timothy 3:16-17, it says, "All Scripture is God-breathed and is useful for teaching, rebuking, correcting and training in righteousness, so that the servant of God may be thoroughly equipped for every good work."

Blessings

The Bible speaks extensively about blessings, both spiritual and material. In Ephesians 1:3, it says, "Praise be to the God and Father of our Lord Jesus Christ, who has blessed us in the heavenly realms with every spiritual blessing in Christ."

Givings

Generosity and giving are commended in the Bible as acts of obedience and love towards God and others. In Luke 6:38, it says, "Give, and it will be given to you. A good measure, pressed down, shaken together and running over, will be poured into your lap. For with the measure you use, it will be measured to you."

Counseling and support:

While the Bible doesn't specifically mention counseling in the modern sense, it does emphasize the importance of seeking wise counsel and support from others. In Proverbs 11:14, it says, "For lack of guidance a nation falls, but victory is won through many advisers."

Leadership training:

The Bible provides examples and principles for leadership throughout its pages, particularly in the lives of figures like Moses, David, and the apostles. In 2 Timothy 2:2, it says, "And the things you have heard me say in the presence of many witnesses entrust to reliable people who will also be qualified to teach others."

Financial literacy workshop:

financial literacy workshops, it provide guidance on stewardship, managing resources wisely, and being responsible with finances. In Proverbs 21:5, it says, "The plans of the diligent lead to profit as surely as haste leads to poverty."
